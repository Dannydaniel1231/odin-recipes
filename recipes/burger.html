Ingredients required to make Classic Burger

  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1½ pounds ground beef, at least 15 percent fat
  • 4 slices American cheese
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 large yellow onion (about 1 pound), peeled and cut into ¼-inch rounds
  • ½ teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons canola or vegetable oil
  • 4 potato rolls or soft hamburger buns, split

These are steps used to make a Smash Burger

  1. Preheat a cast-iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat.
  2. Divide the ground beef into 8 equal portions and shape each portion into a ball.
  3. Place a ball of beef onto the hot skillet and use a spatula to smash it down into a thin patty.
  4. Sprinkle salt, black pepper, and garlic powder on top of the patty.
  5. Cook the patty for 1-2 minutes, then flip it over and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes.
  6. Place a slice of cheese on top of the patty and cook for an additional 30 seconds to melt the cheese.
  7. Remove the patty from the skillet and repeat with the remaining balls of beef.
  8. In a small bowl, mix together mayonnaise and yellow mustard to make the burger sauce.
  9. Toast the burger buns on the skillet for 1-2 minutes.
  10. Assemble the burgers by spreading the sauce on the bottom bun, adding shredded lettuce, sliced tomato, and sliced red onion, placing the patty on top, and adding sliced dill pickles on top of the patty.
  11. Top with the other half of the bun and serve immediately.
