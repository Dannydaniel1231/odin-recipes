Pizza

Description

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin that consists of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with
olive oil, tomatoes, and mozzarella cheese, and in its variations, other toppings, baked quickly,
sliced into segments, and served hot. .The bottom base of the pizza,
called the “crust,” may vary widely according to style, such as thin as in hand-tossed pizza
or Roman pizza, thick as in pan pizza or Chicago-style pizzaThe toppings of pizza
can be varied according to region, culture, or personal preference,
and there are many types of meat, vegetables, and cheese that can be used

Some of the most popular pizza varieties include Margherita, Neapolitan, New York-style, and Sicilian
In general, pizza in Italy is lighter than in the United States, with thinner crusts and fewer toppings

Ingredients required to make Pizza

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp active dry yeast
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Pizza Sauce:
  • 1 can (28 oz) whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Pizza Toppings:
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion

These are steps used to make aPizza

  1. Pizza Dough:
  2. Combine flour, salt, sugar, yeast, warm water, and olive oil in a stand mixer bowl.
  3. Whisk together with a 6-Wire Whip Attachment and let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Knead the dough with a dough hook attachment for 8-10 minutes.
  5. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1-2 hours.
  6. Punch down the dough and roll it out to the desired thickness.
  7. Pizza Sauce:
  8. Blend whole peeled tomatoes in a food processor until smooth.
  9. Heat olive oil in a saucepan and add the tomato puree.
  10. Add salt, sugar, dried oregano, and black pepper.
  11. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes until thickened.
  12. Pizza Toppings:
  13. Preheat the oven to 450°F.
  14. Spread the pizza sauce over the dough.
  15. Add shredded mozzarella cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, chopped fresh basil, pepperoni, black olives, green bell pepper, and red onion.
  16. Bake the pizza for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
